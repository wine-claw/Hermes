About

This app simulates cross-flow membrane filtration for wine clarification. Adjust transmembrane pressure, velocity, pore size, temperature and feed turbidity to observe fouling dynamics, cake layer growth, and permeate flux decline in real time. Use backpulse cleaning to restore membrane performance.

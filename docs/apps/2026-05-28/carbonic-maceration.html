🔬 Process Stages

1. Anaerobic envelope: CO₂ displaces O₂; berries breathe internally.
2. Enzymatic catabolism: Berry enzymes convert 1–2.5% sugar → ethanol + malic acid inside intact cells.
3. Skin weakening: CO₂ + ethanol dissolve pectin; berry turgor drops.
4. Bursting: Weak berries rupture, releasing juice for yeast fermentation.
5. Press: Remaining clusters pressed; blended ferment continues.

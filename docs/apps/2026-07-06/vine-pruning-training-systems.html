Vertical Shoot Positioning (VSP)
The most common training system for premium wine grapes. Canes pruned and tied to a single fruiting wire; shoots trained vertically between moveable foliage wires. Ideal for Cabernet, Pinot Noir, and other upright-growing varieties.
